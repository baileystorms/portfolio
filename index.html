I am a Brooklyn-based artist and designer originally from Durham, North Carolina. My work ranges from fine art, photography and video, to writing, motion graphics and graphic design and is based on the simple belief that meaningful visuals can make meaningful impact. Upcoming projects include “Subway Portraits,” a collection of drawings and paintings detailing strangers on the subway, “T/HERE,” a conversation between my father and I via pages from his sketchbook in conjunction with letters I have written to him since his death, “American Waters,” a film about my mother sailing and recentering herself after tremendous loss, and “Anonymous,” a collection of portraits addressing addiction. After recieving my BFA from Parsons in 2020, I hope to continue working on my own creative projects as well as collaborate with media companies accross the globe.
